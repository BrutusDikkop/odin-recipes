Mexican Burger

Description

A sandwich with a flavorful Mexican-style beef and corn burger, served with guacamole and tomato salsa as a fresh and tasty topping.

Steps

  • First, prepare the tomato salsa so it has time to marinate. Cut the tomato into pieces and remove the watery seeds and pulp. Dice the remaining flesh. Finely chop the onion and mix it with the tomato pieces. Finely chop the coriander and garlic as well, and stir them into the tomatoes together with the oil. Cover the salsa and place it in the refrigerator.
  • Mix the cumin, salt, pepper, garlic, and paprika powder into the ground beef. Add the egg and breadcrumbs and mix until you have a firm mixture. If it is still very sticky, add a little extra breadcrumbs. Drain the corn well and knead it into the meat mixture so it is evenly distributed. Shape the mixture into 4 balls and press them flat to form burgers. Cover and refrigerate the burgers.
  • Cut the avocado in half around the pit and twist the halves apart. Remove the pit and peel, then mash the flesh with a fork. Squeeze the lime and add the juice immediately to prevent the avocado from browning. Finely chop the red chili pepper and mix it into the guacamole.
  • Heat some oil or butter in a pan and cook the burgers for about 10 minutes until nicely browned and cooked through. Meanwhile, slice the buns in half and top with some lettuce. Place the burgers on top and add a slice of cheddar cheese. Spoon a generous amount of guacamole and some tomato salsa over the burgers. Serve immediately.
  • Tip: Delicious served with some nacho chips on the side.

Ingredients

  1. 400 g ground beef
  2. 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  3. 1 teaspoon paprika powder
  4. 140 g corn (bonduella)
  5. 1 clove of garlic
  6. a pinch of salt and peper
  7. 1 egg
  8. 3 tablespoons breadcrumbs
  9. 2 handfuls of lettuce
  10. 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  11. 4 crusty bread rolls
  12. 1 tablespoon oil (for frying)

    13. Tomato Salsa

  13. 3 fresh tomatoes
  14. 1 red onion
  15. 1 clove of garlic
  16. fresh corioander (to taste)
  17. 1 tablespoon olive oil

    18. Guacamole

  18. 2 avocados
  19. 1 red chilli peper (to taste)
  20. 1 lime
