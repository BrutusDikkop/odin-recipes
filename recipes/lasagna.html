Lasagna

width="1021" height="584"

Description

When I’m in the mood for true comfort food, I make this classic Italian lasagna Bolognese with layers of creamy béchamel sauce, flavorful meat sauce, and perfectly cooked lasagna sheets. I love making it myself because it’s surprisingly easy and so much tastier than a store-bought version. Even when I was less experienced in the kitchen, this recipe always turned out well. It’s perfect when you want to prepare something hearty and satisfying that everyone will enjoy. You can also prepare it in advance and simply bake it later, which is especially convenient on busy days. Making lasagna from scratch has never been this simple and delicious.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C.
  2. Finely chop the onion and garlic. Sauté them in a large pan with 1 tablespoon of oil.
  3. Cut the carrot and celery into small pieces and add them to the pan.
  4. After a few minutes, add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Add the oregano and thyme together with the tomato paste.
  6. Then add the diced tomatoes and strained tomatoes, and crumble in the stock cube. Let the lasagna Bolognese sauce simmer gently for approximately 30 minutes.
  7. Season the sauce to taste with a pinch of salt and pepper, if desired.
  8. Meanwhile, prepare the béchamel sauce. Melt the butter in a small saucepan and add the flour. Cook this roux gently for about 3 minutes.
  9. Gradually add the milk while whisking continuously until a smooth sauce forms. Note: the béchamel sauce thickens quickly, so keep stirring well.
  10. Season the béchamel sauce with a little salt, pepper, and optionally nutmeg.
  11. Slice the mozzarella into thin slices.
  12. Grease the baking dish with a little oil or butter.
  13. Spread a small amount of tomato sauce over the bottom of the dish. Place 3 lasagna sheets on top. Add a layer of tomato sauce, then spread some béchamel sauce over it. Place a few slices of mozzarella on top. Cover with more lasagna sheets and repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of tomato sauce, béchamel sauce, and mozzarella.
  14. Sprinkle the lasagna Bolognese with grated cheese and bake in the oven for 45 minutes.
  15. Remove the lasagna from the oven and check whether the pasta sheets are fully cooked. Let it rest for a few minutes to firm up slightly before cutting into portions.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • tablespoons oil
  • 200 g carrot
  • 125 g celery
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 small can of tomato paste (70 g)
  • 400 ml strained tomatoes (canned) (Heinz)
  • 400 g diced tomatoes (canned) (Heinz)
  • 1 beef stock cube (Maggi)
  • A pinch of salt and pepper
  • 2 balls of mozzarella
  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • A handful of grated cheese
